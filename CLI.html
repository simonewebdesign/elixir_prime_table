    elixir_prime_table v0.1.0 CLI

    A command line interface. The executable will accept only one numeric argument that is greater than zero and will print a primes multiplication table.

    Examples 

    $ bin/prime-table 2
|   | 2 | 3 |
| 2 | 4 | 6 |
| 3 | 6 | 9 |
